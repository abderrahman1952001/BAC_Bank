# SVT M canonical curriculum

This is the platform-authored layer for `علوم الطبيعة والحياة` in the
`رياضيات` stream.

Subject-agnostic curriculum rules live in `../../README.md`.

For V1, the M stream uses a strict slice of the SE SVT curriculum plus the
older M environment-management field that appears in historical papers:

- `PROTEINS`
  - `PROTEIN_SYNTHESIS`
  - `STRUCTURE_FUNCTION`
  - `IMMUNITY`
- `HUMAN_AND_PLANET_MANAGEMENT`
  - `AIR_POLLUTION`
  - `WATER_POLLUTION`

Do not reuse the full SE energy or tectonics spine for M. M mappings should
report out-of-scope exercise candidates instead of silently tagging them to
missing or SE-only units.
