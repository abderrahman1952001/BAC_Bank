## Why This Fails

- It invents hype instead of teaching.
- It says `u_1` is always the first term, which is false.
- It gives a formula without stating the allowed values of `n`.
- It has no real BAC move.
- The quiz checks nothing.
- It sounds like a motivational theme park, not premium education.
